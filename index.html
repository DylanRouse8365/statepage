Nebraska At A Glance

Life In Nebraska

Main Types of Harverst

Corn harvest is very common in Nebraska. Corn harvest commonly occurs around fall. There is also wheat harvest which happens way before corn harvest but is still very common. Soybeans is another type of crop that is harvest here in Nebraska but not as common as Corn and Wheat. During harvest season, it is very important to use caustion when traveling backroads. There is a lot more traffic wheather it be semis or tractors that are driving from fields and to where they store their crop.

Sandhill Cranes

Sanshill cranes are a very large bird that thousands of people travel to go see once a year. Every year, Sandhill cranes all gather around the Kearney, Nebraska area. there can be thousands of birds in one area at the same time.

Sunsets In Nebraska

Sunsets in Nebraska can be very cool almost every night. Changing eery second, it can be hard to capture how amazing the sunsets can be in Nebraska. The colors of the sunsets all depends on the time of year, weather, and the amount of clouds in the sky. Some nights there might not be much of a sunset and some nights the sky will be many different colors.

Nebraska Landscape

Mainy people think that Nebraska is completley flat and all you can see is fields for miles. This is true is some parts of Nebraska but it is far from being flat. There are many areas where there are many trees and several large hills in one area. If you want to see a lot of flat land with hardly any trees, take a trip to Denver, Colorado and be ready to experience the longest, boring drive ever. This drive will really show you how unique the terrain is here in Nebraska.
